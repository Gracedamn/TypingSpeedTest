# TypingSpeedTest
Are you spending way too much time on your phone everyday? Remember, a real Computer Science student belongs at the keyboard! Your typing speed is the key to beating course deadlines and finishing assignments faster than your peers. Check out this typing speed test to measure your WPM (Words per Minutes) and typing accuracy. Choose your desired difficulty, or test your nerves with intense Timed Challenge and Sudden Death modes!

Track your progress with the integrated leaderboard, every attempt is saved into a txt file, all players' stats are stored in the same directory path as the python program, so your history is always ready to be retrieved for your next time play.
